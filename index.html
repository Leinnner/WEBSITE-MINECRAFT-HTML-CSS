Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang. The game was created by Markus "Notch" Persson in the Java programming language. It was later developed by a team at Mojang, and was released as a public alpha for personal computers in 2009 before officially releasing in November 2011, with Jens Bergensten taking over development.
